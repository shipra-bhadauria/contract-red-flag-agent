EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into as of January 1, 2025, 
between TechCorp Pvt Ltd ("Company") and the undersigned ("Employee").

1. POSITION
Employee will serve as Software Engineer. Company reserves the right 
to change Employee's role at any time without notice or compensation.

2. COMPENSATION
Monthly salary of INR 50,000. Company may change compensation at 
its sole discretion without prior notice to Employee.

3. NON-COMPETE
Employee agrees not to work for any competitor for 3 years after 
termination. This applies worldwide across all industries.

4. TERMINATION
Company may terminate Employee at any time without notice, reason, 
or severance payment.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work created by Employee, including personal projects created 
outside working hours, shall be property of the Company.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee must keep all company information confidential for 10 years 
after termination.
